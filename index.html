How it works

Simple for you.
Powerful for families.

Four steps from your blog to their dinner table.

1

You get listed

We add your blog to the Blogger Cookbook. Your name, specialty, and recipes featured in the app for families to follow.

2

Families follow you

When a family's taste profile matches your cuisine — Italian, healthy, quick dinners — Fern recommends your blog. They tap Follow.

3

AI generates your recipes

A family taps one of your recipes. Fern generates the full recipe in your style — ingredients, instructions, tips. Your brand on every step.

4

They cook, you get credit

The recipe goes into their weekly planner, their shopping list, their Cook Mode. "From: Your Blog" on every single touchpoint.
